About Me


My name is Lewis and I am a freelance frontend-focused web developer. I really enjoy designing and developing websites and am trying to make a living out of it to support my family.

I attended the University of Kent for my undergraduate degree in Computer Science (Hons), where I graduated First Class. After that, I completed an MA in Games Design at Canterbury Christ Church University. The combinatino of these two subjects allowed me to channel my creativity through programming. I am always trying to learn new things every day and create cool looking things with it.

Whilst on my undergraduate degree, I learned so much about many different faucets of Computer Science, such as:

  • Computational creativity
  • Algorithms
  • Databases
  • Neural Networks
  • Mobile Development
  • Software Development

However, in my final year of my undergraduate degree, I took a module in Game Development, and I fell in love. I had always really enjoyed getting lost in digital games, and this was my chance to see the creative process of developing them. I also massively excelled at it, and after my degree was finished I could feel a game design-shaped hole in my heart, so I pursued a Masters in it.

I spent a long time around about the time of my Masters using Unity to create games. For my final project I created a VR game where the player competes in a wizard duel. They would use the VR controllers as a wand and depending on how they waved it, different spells would cast. I completed this project entirely by myself which involved:

  • Creating 3D models
  • Programming
  • Using the Unity engine to develop the game
  • Playtesting
  • Sound design

I really enjoy game design and programming, however it is rather hard to find a paid, stable job as a beginner, and the pressure of the arrival of my son Atlas forced me to pursue a more imminently possible career. So I went back to my roots from Computer Science. I didn't particularly study website creation in University, however I have embarked on a course over the past year learning website programming and have taken quite an interest to website creation and optimisation. It has a fantastic balance of creativity and efficient programming.
